# WorkNest Landing Page Design Plan

## Design Direction
- **Typography**: Cursive/Script fonts for headings
- **Style**: Pastel colors with elegant, sophisticated feel
- **Approach**: Step-by-step implementation

## Color Palette (Pastel & Elegant)
- **Primary**: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA)
- **Secondary**: Pale mint (#F0FFF0) 
- **Accent**: Dusty rose (#F5C2C7)
- **Neutral**: Warm cream (#FDF6E3)
- **Text**: Charcoal gray (#36454F)
- **Highlights**: Soft gold (#F7E7CE)

## Typography Plan
- **Headings**: Sorts Mill Goudy (elegant serif) - `.sorts-mill-goudy-regular` / `.sorts-mill-goudy-regular-italic`
- **Body Text**: Funnel Sans (modern sans-serif) - `.funnel-sans-regular`, `.funnel-sans-medium`, etc.
- **Creative Elements**: Yarndings 20 (decorative symbols) - `.yarndings-20-regular`
- **Separators**: Yarndings for elegant dividers and decorative touches
