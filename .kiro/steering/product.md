# WorkNest Product Overview

WorkNest is an AI-powered HR workflow automation platform built during the Kiro Hackathon. It streamlines repetitive HR operations by connecting HR systems, AI, and workplace tools into unified workflows.

## Core Purpose
- Automate onboarding processes
- Handle employee surveys and forms
- Generate performance reports
- Track attendance
- Provide real-time status updates

## Key Integrations
- **Slack API** - Team communication and notifications
- **Google Workspace API** - Form syncing and data management
- **Kiro Hooks** - Event-driven automation triggers
- **MCP (Multi-Context Processing)** - Context-aware task execution

## Architecture Philosophy
WorkNest follows a modular, event-driven architecture where Kiro Hooks detect HR events, MCP processes context and invokes AI modules, and outputs are distributed to integrated platforms (Slack, Google Sheets, dashboards).

## Target Users
HR teams looking to reduce manual work through intelligent automation and AI-powered insights.
